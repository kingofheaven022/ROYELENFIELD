About Bullet 350

Royal Enfield Bullet 350 is a cruiser bike available at a starting price of Rs. 1,47,910 in India. It is available in 3 variants and 6 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 1,63,217. The Royal Enfield Bullet 350 is powered by 346cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 19.1 bhp and a torque of 28 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Royal Enfield Bullet 350 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This Bullet 350 bike weighs 191 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 13.5 liters.

Royal Enfield (India)

Royal Enfield is an Indian multinational motorcycle manufacturing company headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. The company is the oldest global motorcycle brand in continuous production,[3] and operates manufacturing plants in Chennai in India. The first Royal Enfield motorcycle was built in 1901 by the Enfield Cycle Company of England, which was responsible for the design and original production of the Royal Enfield Bullet, the longest-lived motorcycle design in history.[4] Licensed from Royal Enfield by the indigenous Indian Madras Motors, the company is now a subsidiary of Eicher Motors Limited, an Indian automaker.[5]
